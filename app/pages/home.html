Final Competition 2017







What is the Final Competition?


Pioneers in Engineering's Final Competition is the last event of the robotics competition season. This year, we had over 200 high school students from 18 high schools competing to see who could build the best robot. The Final Competition is where they finally get to pit their robots against each other to win it all in this year's game, Dry Another Day.




Who are we?


We are Pioneers in Engineering (PiE), a student-run organization that promotes science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) education through an exciting, mentorship-based process. We believe that no student should be denied an education in STEM.


If you are interested and want to learn more, check us out at our website.
